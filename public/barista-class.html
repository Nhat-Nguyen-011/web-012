BASIC BARISTA TRAINING PROGRAM

$850 PER PAX, 8 HOURS

If you are aspiring to be a barista, then this course is one that you do not want to miss out. The class comprises of Espresso extraction techniques, milk frothing and texturing technique and how to pour a balanced tasting cup of cafe latte and cafe cappuccino. There would be ample hands on time during the training class with trainers guidance in a relaxed and friendly environment. ADVANCE BARISTA TRAINING PROGRAM

$1200 PER PAX, 12 HOURS

This intense 15 hours course is designed for Baristas who are looking to refine their skills and technique and take it to the next level. The focus is mainly on revisiting and fine tune various technique such as espresso extraction and milk frothing technique.
